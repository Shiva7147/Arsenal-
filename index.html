About the Supporters Club

The home of Bengaluru’s Arsenal Family

Arsenal Supporters Club Bengaluru (ASCB) brings together passionate Gunners across the city to celebrate every matchday, every goal, and every unforgettable Arsenal moment. From screenings and chants to turf games and community events, this is where Bengaluru’s Arsenal family comes together.

Founded by fans who wanted to replicate the high-octane atmosphere of North London and the Emirates Stadium, ASCB has grown from a handful of fans in a pub to a structured community that coordinates gatherings, tournaments, charity drives, and official supporter events.

Whether you are a lifelong supporter who remembers the Invincibles or a new fan experiencing the thrill of Mikel Arteta's exciting squad, you have a home with us.

Connect with the Bengaluru Red Army

Have questions about match venue entry, membership registration, or turf games? Click any FAQ item below or send us a message using the form.

How do I attend a pub screening? +
Simply navigate to our Screenings tab, select an upcoming match, click 'RSVP Seat', and generate your digital Matchday Pass. Show this pass at the venue entrance.
Is there cover charge entry for pub screenings? +
Yes, screening venues in Bengaluru (like Church Street Social and Doffpub) usually collect a cover charge which can be fully redeemed on food and beverages inside.
How do I play in the weekend turf matches? +
Register under our Match Day tab. We announce registrations every Monday. Slots are allocated on a first-come, first-served basis for verified members.
